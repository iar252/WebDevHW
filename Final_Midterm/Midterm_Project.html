Hello! This blog is basically for those who loved the Bollywood movies they watched growing up as kids. I think we all feel that little tug at our hearts when we remember the songs we danced to and the scenes we cried to. I'll be reviewing different movies with a perspective of a young adult instead of the child who watched these movies, so expect lots of sarcasm. You'll notice a lot of the movies are Shahrukh Khan movies and thats basically because...well who doesn't love the King of Bollywood.
Sidenote: If you were never exposed to Bollywood, I summarize the movies as well so you can read about them and get the gist of it instead of watching it (who reads a summary instead of watching the movie though...unless its Harry Potter or Percy Jackson) Anyway, enjoy my reviews!
